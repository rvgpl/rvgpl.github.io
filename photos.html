Through practice, I’ve reach the point where I’ve gain confidence in presenting myself as a photographer. I continue to learn new techniques everyday and getting comfortable with the intricacies of operating the camera.


To see more of my photography, head over to my photo blog or flickr. Do check out my instagram feed for mobile photography.


Camera Gear

I use a Nikon D5200 with Tamron 17-50mm f/2.8, Tamrom 70-300mm f/4-5.6 and Nikon 50mm f/1.8 lenses. The D5200 boasts 24 megapixels which is very large and it is more sensitive to light with decent low-light performace. I practice photography using old films cameras, currently using Pentax K1000 with 50mm f/2.0 lens.


Accessories

I use UV Filters from Hoya on all my lenses to protect them from everything. I have Cokin P Series ND and GND filters which I use rarely as similar effect can be acheived in Lightroom, attaching and removing this type of filters is a pain. I do use them for recording videos during bright conditions though.


I use Sandisk Extreme Pro 16gb cards for storage and backup raw photos to a 1TB Western Digital external hard drive. For shhoting landscapes or for video recording, I carry a Dolica AX620B100 with a ball head or fluid head depending on my needs. I have a Nissin Di866 external flash which I use rarely as it adds weight to the camera and carrying it around adds pain to my wrists.


I carry all this in a Lowepro Flipside 400 AW camera backpack, it is quiet spacious and allows me to carry all my gear properly with nice padding and protection.
